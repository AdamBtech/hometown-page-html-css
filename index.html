Visit Suresnes

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to Paris.

Top three activities to do at Suresnes

Mont Valérien

Visit this historic place for its breathtaking views of Paris and its role as a memorial site, honoring French soldiers and resistance fighters executed during World War II.

Musée d'histoire urbaine

Delve into the rich urban and social history of Suresnes through captivating exhibits and interactive displays.

Parc du Château de Suresnes

Enjoy a serene escape at a picturesque park surrounding the Château de Suresnes
